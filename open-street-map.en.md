[Open Street Map](https://www.openstreetmap.org) (OSM) is a free, community built digital map of the world, supported by the [OpenStreetMap Foundation](https://osmfoundation.org/) and other organizations and companies.

The OSM ecosystem is the alternative to proprietary mapping services.
